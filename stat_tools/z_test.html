Important classroom notes

The alternative hypothesis should be specified before interpreting the result.

Caution: the three decision lines shown above are three different hypothesis decisions, not three simultaneous truths.

This simplified classroom template uses sample variance as an approximation to population variance.

The calculation also assumes that the underlying data distribution is normal, or that the sample setting is suitable for normal approximation.
