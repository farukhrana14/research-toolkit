📂 Cited Articles Submission Guideline

Step 1 of 7

Before You Begin

Read this carefully before starting. Every step is mandatory.

  • Use your @university.edu.bd Google account for everything. Sign out of personal Gmail first if needed.
  • Upload only journal articles you have directly cited in your literature review. Every cited article must have a PDF in the folder.
  • Each group uploads approximately 25 articles.

Part 1 — Collect Your Articles as PDF Files

🗂 Have you already downloaded all your articles as PDF files?

Step 1 — Google Scholar

Go to scholar.google.com and search the article title. If a [PDF] link appears to the right, click it and download. If not, continue below.

Step 2 — JSTOR via University Library

Sign in with your @university.edu.bd account. Access JSTOR through your university library portal. Search and download the PDF if available.

Step 3 — ResearchGate

Go to researchgate.net, search the exact article title. If a Download button is available, download the PDF.

Step 4 — Check Journal Free Access Programs

Find the journal's official website. Look for sections titled Open Access, Developing Countries Access, Research4Life, or HINARI. Bangladesh qualifies for several such programs. Register and download if available.

Step 5 — Email the Author

Find the author's email on the abstract page or ResearchGate. Send this message:

Subject: Request for Article PDF

Dear Dr. [Last Name],

I am a BBA student at [University Name]. I am conducting research and would greatly benefit from reading your article "[Full Article Title]" published in [Journal Name, Year]. Could you kindly share a PDF copy?

Thank you,
[Your Name]

Step 6 — Save an HTML Article as PDF

If the journal displays the article as a webpage with no download button:

  1. Press Ctrl + P (Windows) or Cmd + P (Mac) on the article page.
  2. In the Destination/Printer field, select Save as PDF or Microsoft Print to PDF.
  3. Click Save, name the file correctly (see Part 2), and save to your computer.
⚠️ Last Resort: If none of the above works, replace that article with one you can access freely through Google Scholar, JSTOR, or open-access journals.

Part 2 — Name Every PDF File Correctly

Rename every file before uploading using this exact format:

PublicationYear_AuthorLastName_AuthorFirstName.pdf

Rules

  • Use the year the article was published, not the download year.
  • Last name first, then first name, separated by underscore.
  • If two or more authors (or et al.), use only the first author's last and first name.
  • No spaces. Underscores only. No special characters, commas, or brackets.

Examples

Article CitationCorrect File Name
Rahman, T. & Karim, S. (2021)2021_Rahman_Tariq.pdf
Smith, J. (2019)2019_Smith_John.pdf
Hossain et al. (2023)2023_Hossain_Farhan.pdf

How to Rename

  1. Right-click the PDF file on your computer.
  2. Click Rename.
  3. Type the correct name and press Enter.

Part 3 — Create the Folder Structure on Google Drive

Go to drive.google.com. Confirm you are signed in with your @university.edu.bd account.

  1. Click + New → New Folder → name it TeamSunrise → click Create.
  2. Open the TeamSunrise folder.
  3. Click + New → New Folder → name it LiteratureReview → click Create.
  4. Open LiteratureReview.
  5. Click + New → New Folder → name it Articles → click Create.

Your folder path must be exactly:

TeamSunrise / LiteratureReview / Articles

Part 4 — Upload All PDF Files

  1. Open the Articles folder on Google Drive.
  2. Click + New → File Upload.
  3. Select all renamed PDF files and click Open.
  4. Wait until all files finish uploading. Do not close the browser tab while uploading.
  5. Count the files to confirm all articles are present.
✅ All PDFs must be inside Articles — not in LiteratureReview or TeamSunrise directly.

Part 5 — Share the Folder

Share the TeamSunrise folder — the outermost one, not the inner folders.

  1. Go to your Google Drive main page.
  2. Click the TeamSunrise folder once to select it (do not open it).
  3. Click the Share button.
  4. In the sharing window, find the "Add people and groups" text box.
  5. Type teacher@university.edu.bd → press Enter → set permission to Editor → click Send.
  6. Repeat for every group member email listed below.
Group members to share with:

your group members' emails
⛔ Do NOT click "Copy link." Type each email address individually in the Share window. A copied link is not acceptable.
✅ Google automatically sends a notification email to each person you add. No separate message needed.

Final Checklist

Tick every item. Your submission is complete only when all boxes are checked.

  • Using @university.edu.bd Google account
  • All articles are directly cited in the literature review
  • Every PDF renamed in Year_LastName_FirstName.pdf format
  • First author used for multi-author articles
  • Folder structure: TeamSunrise/LiteratureReview/Articles
  • All PDFs are inside the Articles folder
  • The TeamSunrise folder (outermost) is shared
  • teacher@university.edu.bd added with Editor access
  • All group members added with Editor access
  • Shared by typing emails directly — not by copying a link
✅ Tick all boxes above to unlock the Finish button.

You did it! 🎉

Your literature review articles have been collected, named, organised, and shared on Google Drive. Submission complete.
